Why "Peacock"?

The Mating Analogy

In nature, the male peacock spreads his tail to showcase his unique health and abilities. The female then selects the most eye-catching performer.

Our Concept: The actor represents the male, highlighting their uniqueness until the casting office (female) selects them for the masterpiece.

Values: Elegance | Distinction | Modernity
